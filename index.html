MICHELIN's Top-Rated Restaurants in France

By Zachary Carnell

Information Source: MICHELIN Guide

This map displays the locations of the 31 restaurants across France that have received the esteemed honor of three MICHELIN stars. The MICHELIN Guide is a leading source on the quality of restaurant experiences all around the world. Its three-star rating system allows consumers to determine which are the best of the best. France, in particular, has more MICHELIN stars than any other country.
